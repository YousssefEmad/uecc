About the Product

The UECC Hydraulic Test Bench is engineered for professional technicians who demand accuracy, durability, and efficiency. Built with advanced pressure monitoring and flow control systems, this equipment allows detailed diagnostics and calibration of hydraulic components in both light and heavy-duty machinery.

Ideal for use in automotive service centers, manufacturing workshops, and industrial maintenance facilities, the HTB-500 offers customizable test parameters, user-friendly operation, and reliable data output for comprehensive analysis.

With robust construction and smart safety features, UECC’s hydraulic solutions help engineers reduce downtime, improve testing precision, and extend the lifespan of industrial equipment — making it a trusted choice across multiple industries.
